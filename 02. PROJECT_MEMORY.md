# Project Memory

## Decision

The player is called the Keeper.

Reason

The player protects balance instead of becoming a hero.

---

## Decision

Daily Challenge is named Daily Anomaly.

Reason

Supports immersion.

---

## Decision

The Universe Hub is not a menu.

Reason

It is part of the living Universe.

---

## Decision

Progress is shown through visible change.

Reason

Players should understand the Universe without reading text.

---

## Decision

Five Laws renamed to Five Orders.

Reason

Order better represents the philosophical foundation of Solitaire Universe.

Variants are expressions of Orders.

Virtues are what the Keeper learns through restoring them.
