# Solitaire Universe

# Visual Language

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the visual identity of Solitaire Universe.

Visual design is storytelling.

Every color, shape, and light source should reinforce the living Universe.

---

# Philosophy

Beauty exists to communicate meaning.

Nothing is purely decorative.

Every visual element should explain the current state of the Universe.

---

# Color Language

## Black

Infinity.

Silence.

Space.

Potential.

---

## Dark Gold

Dormant energy.

A Law waiting to be restored.

---

## Bright Gold

Balance restored.

Hope.

Life.

---

## White

Truth.

Clarity.

Guidance.

Used sparingly.

---

# Light

Light represents restoration.

More progress means more light.

Brightness should always be earned.

---

# Shapes

Diamond

The Core.

Origin.

Unity.

---

Circle

Continuity.

Balance.

The Universe.

---

Lines

Connection.

Energy flow.

Relationships.

---

Stars

Possibility.

Distance.

Time.

---

# World Progress

Worlds should visibly evolve.

Players should recognize progress before reading percentages.

Brightness.

Chapter Rings.

Core Glow.

Energy Lines.

All should reflect restoration.

---

# UI Philosophy

The interface should disappear.

Players should feel they are interacting with the Universe,

not software.

---

# Negative Space

Empty space is intentional.

Silence has visual value.

The Universe should never feel crowded.

---

# Intro Principle

Visuals should reveal,

never explain.

The player discovers meaning through observation.

---

# Design Validation

Ask:

Does this visual communicate something?

Would removing it reduce understanding?

Does it strengthen immersion?

---

# One Sentence

Every light in the Universe tells part of the story.
