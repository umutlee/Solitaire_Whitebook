# Solitaire Universe

# Creative Pillars

Version 1.0 (Canon)

---

# Purpose

Creative Pillars define the identity of Solitaire Universe.

They are the foundation for every design decision.

Features may evolve.

Technology may change.

The Pillars remain.

Whenever a new idea is proposed, it should strengthen at least one Pillar without contradicting another.

---

# Pillar I

## A Living Universe

The Universe exists independently of the player.

The player does not create the Universe.

The player enters it.

Worlds continue to exist.

The Core continues to breathe.

Anomalies continue to emerge.

Returning should always feel like coming back to a place that has quietly continued without you.

---

# Pillar II

## Meaningful Progress

Every action should leave a visible mark.

Progress is not only stored in data.

Progress is reflected throughout the Universe.

The Core grows brighter.

Worlds awaken.

Chapter Rings illuminate.

The Universe remembers every restoration.

Players should feel that every completed Run contributed to something larger than itself.

---

# Pillar III

## Quiet Wonder

Wonder is created through restraint.

Silence is more powerful than constant spectacle.

Small changes become meaningful when surrounded by stillness.

The Universe should inspire curiosity rather than overwhelm the player.

Beauty should invite observation instead of demanding attention.

---

# Pillar IV

## Respect Player Time

The player's time is valuable.

Returning should always feel rewarding.

Leaving should never be punished.

Daily Anomalies invite the Keeper back,

but never demand attendance.

The Universe welcomes every return,

regardless of how much time has passed.

---

# Pillar V

## Elegant Simplicity

Everything the player sees should feel effortless.

Complexity belongs beneath the surface.

Rules may be sophisticated.

Systems may be advanced.

The experience should always remain calm,

clear,

and intuitive.

True elegance is achieved when depth becomes invisible.

---

# Decision Filter

Before implementing a new feature, ask:

Does it strengthen the Living Universe?

Does it create meaningful progress?

Does it preserve Quiet Wonder?

Does it respect the player's time?

Does it maintain elegant simplicity?

If the answer is "no" to several of these questions,

the feature should be reconsidered.

---

# Relationship to the Bible

The Bible explains what the Universe is.

These Pillars explain how Solitaire Universe should always feel.

Every future document should align with these principles.

---

# One Sentence

Solitaire Universe is not defined by the Solitaire games it contains,

but by the feelings players carry with them after they leave.
