# Solitaire Universe

# Motion Language

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines how the Universe moves.

Motion is not decoration.

Every animation should communicate life, balance, or restoration.

If movement exists without purpose, it should be removed.

---

# Philosophy

The Universe is ancient.

It does not rush.

Nothing moves to impress the player.

Everything moves because the Universe is alive.

Motion should create trust, not excitement.

---

# Core Principles

## Slow Before Fast

Movement should begin gently.

Acceleration should feel natural.

Sudden motion should be rare and meaningful.

---

## Breathing

The Core breathes.

Worlds breathe.

Light breathes.

Particles breathe.

Breathing is the visual expression of life.

---

## Momentum

Nothing appears instantly.

Everything arrives.

Everything leaves.

Transitions should preserve continuity.

---

## Weight

Objects have presence.

Large objects move with greater inertia.

Small particles may drift freely.

Nothing should feel weightless unless intentionally supernatural.

---

## Silence Between Motion

Stillness is part of motion.

Moments without movement allow the player to appreciate the next change.

---

# Motion Hierarchy

## Highest Priority

The Core

Its motion should always be readable.

---

## Second

World Restoration

Brightness, rotation, chapter rings.

---

## Third

Energy Connections

Energy Lines.

Living particles.

Ambient stars.

---

## Fourth

Interface

Panels.

Buttons.

Transitions.

UI should never compete with the Universe.

---

# Gameplay Motion

Card movement should remain clean and precise.

Animations must improve readability.

Never sacrifice responsiveness for visual effects.

The cards are tools.

The Universe is emotion.

---

# Intro Principle

Every movement during the Intro should answer one question:

Why is the Universe changing?

The logo is not animated.

It is assembled.

---

# Design Validation

Before adding animation, ask:

Does this communicate life?

Does it strengthen immersion?

Does it support the emotional journey?

Would removing it make the experience clearer?

---

# One Sentence

Motion is the heartbeat of the living Universe.
