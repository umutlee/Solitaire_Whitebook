# Canon Terminology

Universe

The living star system.

---

Core

The heart of the Universe.

---

Order

A fundamental principle that sustains balance.

---

Variant

A Solitaire ruleset through which an Order is experienced.

---

Virtue

The understanding cultivated by restoring an Order.

---

Keeper

The player.

---

Anomaly

A naturally occurring instability.

---

Distortion

The visible effect of an Anomaly upon an Order.

---

Chapter

A stage of restoring one Order.

---

Run

One attempt within a Chapter.
