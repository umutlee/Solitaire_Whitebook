# The Solitaire Universe Bible

# 04 — The Keeper

Version 0.1 (Draft)

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines the player.

The player is known as the Keeper.

---

# Who is the Keeper?

The Keeper is not a chosen hero.

The Keeper is simply someone who chooses to care.

The Universe does not ask for perfection.

It asks for responsibility.

---

# Responsibility

The Keeper restores the Orders.

Not by force.

Not by destiny.

But one Chapter at a time.

---

# Progress

The Keeper witnesses change.

Every restored World.

Every brighter Core.

Every completed Chapter.

Every resolved Anomaly.

These changes are reminders that every action matters.

---

# The Keeper Never Owns the Universe

The Universe belongs to itself.

The Keeper is only its guardian.

Their role is temporary.

Their responsibility is meaningful.

---

# Design Principles

The player should never feel like a conqueror.

The player should feel like a caretaker.

The Universe is not won.

It is maintained.

---

# One Sentence

The Keeper is the one who chooses to preserve balance. Every player becomes a different Keeper.
