# The Solitaire Universe Bible

# 08 — Universe Hub

Version 1.0 (Canon)

---

# Purpose

The Universe Hub is the living heart of Solitaire Universe.

It is not a menu.

It is where the Keeper witnesses the current state of the Universe.

---

# A Living Observatory

Every visual element reflects reality.

The Core.

The Worlds.

The Chapter Rings.

The Energy Lines.

The Nebula.

Nothing exists purely for decoration.

Everything communicates the health of the Universe.

---

# Visual Storytelling

Players should understand the state of the Universe before reading a single word.

Brightness.

Movement.

Color.

Silence.

Light.

Together,

they tell the story.

---

# Presence

The Universe Hub should always feel calm.

Ancient.

Patient.

Alive.

The Hub waits.

It never rushes the Keeper.

---

# Design Principles

Every animation should communicate life.

Every visual change should communicate progress.

Every interaction should strengthen immersion.

Nothing should exist only because it looks beautiful.

Beauty must always have purpose.

---

# Relationship to Gameplay

Gameplay changes the Hub.

The Hub reflects gameplay.

Neither exists independently.

Together,

they create the feeling of caring for a living Universe.

---

# One Sentence

The Universe Hub is where the player sees the consequences of every choice. The Hub is where the Keeper returns, not where the player starts.
