# The Solitaire Universe Bible

# 06 — Anomalies

Version 1.0 (Canon)

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines Anomalies.

Anomalies are not villains.

They are not creatures.

They are not an opposing force.

They are the natural tendency of the Universe to drift away from balance.

Without Anomalies, there would be nothing to restore.

---

# What is an Anomaly?

Every Universe seeks balance.

Yet balance is never permanent.

Over time, the Orders begin to distort.

Connections weaken.

Patterns become unstable.

Certainty gives way to unpredictability.

These moments are known as Anomalies.

---

# Anomalies are Inevitable

Anomalies are not mistakes.

They are not punishments.

They are not signs that the Universe is failing.

They are a natural consequence of existence.

Just as stars grow dim and rivers change their course,

every living system slowly drifts away from perfect balance.

The Solitaire Universe is no different.

---

# Distortion

An Anomaly never destroys an Order.

Instead,

it bends it.

The Order still exists,

but no longer behaves as expected.

The familiar becomes unfamiliar.

The predictable becomes uncertain.

Players experience this through Distortions.

Every Distortion is a temporary change in how a Law expresses itself.

---

# Stabilization

Anomalies cannot be defeated.

They cannot be erased.

They can only be stabilized.

Stabilization restores balance,

allowing the affected Order to function normally once more.

This is why the Universe records

Distortions Stabilized,

never Distortions Defeated.

The Keeper preserves balance.

The Keeper does not wage war.

---

# Daily Emergence

Because balance is never permanent,

new Anomalies continue to emerge.

Every day,

the Universe reveals new distortions.

Some appear within Klondike.

Others surface in Spider,

FreeCell,

Pyramid,

or TriPeaks.

No two days are exactly alike.

The Universe is always changing.

---

# Relationship to the Five Orders

Every Anomaly distorts one of the Five Orders.  
The Five Orders sustain balance. Anomalies introduce instability.  
The Laws define order.

Neither exists without the other.  
A Universe without Orders would descend into chaos.  
A Universe without Laws would descend into chaos.

A Universe without Anomalies would never change.

Together,

they create a living system that requires constant care.

---

# The Keeper's Responsibility

The Keeper is not expected to eliminate every Anomaly.

That would be impossible.

Instead,

the Keeper answers the call of the present.

Today's disturbances.

Today's imbalance.

Today's responsibility.

By stabilizing the Anomalies of each day,

the Keeper allows the Universe to continue its quiet rhythm.

---

# Design Principles

Anomalies should never feel evil.

They should feel inevitable.

They should create curiosity,

not frustration.

Every Distortion should encourage players to rethink familiar strategies,

rather than simply increasing difficulty.

The player should feel that the Orders are shifting,

not breaking.

---

# Player Experience

Players should never think:

"I have three daily missions."

Instead,

they should feel:

"Three new disturbances have appeared."

"The Universe needs attention today."

"I can help restore its balance."

---

# One Sentence

Anomalies are the Universe's natural drift away from balance, and every day, the Keeper chooses to restore it once more.
