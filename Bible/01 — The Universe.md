# The Solitaire Universe Bible

# 01 — The Universe

Version 1.0 (Draft)

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines what the Universe is.

It is not a story.

It is the foundation upon which every feature, every interaction, and every piece of presentation is built.

If this chapter changes, the entire project changes.

---

# What is the Universe?

The Universe is not a place.

It is a living system.

It endures because the Five Orders remain in balance.

As long as those Orders are maintained, order exists.

When one Order weakens, the balance begins to shift.

When several Orders weaken, the Universe slowly falls into disorder.

The player is not merely completing Solitaire games.

The player is preserving the balance of the Universe.

---

# The Universe is Alive

The Universe is never static.

It constantly reflects the player's actions.

When progress is made,

the Universe responds.

When Worlds are restored,

the Core grows brighter.

When Chapters are completed,

new paths emerge.

When Daily Anomalies appear,

the Universe becomes unstable once again.

Nothing happens without leaving a mark.

---

# The Universe is Persistent

The Universe remembers.

Every completed Chapter.

Every restored World.

Every solved Run.

Every Daily Anomaly.

Every action contributes to the overall state of the Universe.

Progress is not represented only by numbers.

Progress is represented by visible change.

The Universe itself is the save file.

---

# The Universe is Visual

Players should understand the state of the Universe without reading text.

The Hub.

The Core.

The Worlds.

The Chapter Rings.

The Energy Lines.

Together they communicate the health of the Universe.

Visual feedback always comes before explanation.

---

# The Universe Never Ends

Completing Story Mode restores the Universe.

It does not complete it.

The Universe continues to exist.

New Anomalies emerge.

New disturbances appear.

The player is no longer restoring the Universe.

The player becomes its Keeper.

Story Mode marks the end of restoration.

It marks the beginning of stewardship.

---

# Design Principles

Every system inside Solitaire Universe must reinforce one or more of the following principles.

• The Universe is alive.

• The Universe remembers.

• The Universe reacts.

• The Universe can weaken.

• The Universe can recover.

If a feature does not strengthen these principles, it should not become part of the Universe.

---

# Relationship to Gameplay

Gameplay is not separated from the Universe.

Every Solitaire Run represents an act of restoration.

Every completed Chapter strengthens a World.

Every restored World strengthens the Core.

Every Daily Anomaly challenges the balance once again.

The player's actions always have meaning beyond the current match.

---

# What the Player Should Feel

The player should never feel that they are navigating menus.

The player should feel that they are returning to a living Universe.

The player should feel that the Universe exists even when they are away.

The player should feel responsible for maintaining its balance.

Not because they are forced to.

Because they care.

---

# One Sentence

Solitaire Universe is a living Universe sustained by five timeless Orders, waiting for its Keeper.
