# The Solitaire Universe Bible

# 07 — The Living Universe

Version 1.0 (Canon)

---

# Purpose

A Living Universe never repeats itself.

Completion is not the conclusion of Solitaire Universe.

It is simply the beginning of stewardship.

---

# A Living System

The Universe is alive.

It evolves continuously.

Balance is maintained,

never permanently achieved.

Every restoration strengthens the present.

Every Anomaly reminds us that tomorrow will be different.

---

# Beyond Story Mode

The First Restoration teaches the Orders.

Once completed,

the Universe no longer follows a predetermined path.

Its future becomes unknowable.

Random Seeds,

Daily Anomalies,

future expansions,

and every new challenge all emerge from the same living system.

---

# Endless Care

A living Universe never asks for endless grinding.

Instead,

it quietly presents new opportunities to preserve balance.

The Keeper chooses when to answer.

Returning should feel meaningful,

never obligatory.

---

# Design Principles

The Universe should never feel finished.

The player should always feel welcome to return.

Progress should accumulate.

The Universe should remember.

Every visit should feel like continuing an ongoing story,

not restarting one.

---

# Player Experience

Players should leave the game feeling at peace,

knowing the Universe will continue to exist.

When they return,

the Universe has changed,

and once again invites their care.

---

# One Sentence

The Universe never asks the Keeper to stay forever,

only to return when they are ready.
